A Tasty Baked Salmon Recipe

This homemade strawberry shortcake recipe is a delightful and nostalgic dessert that will transport you back to summertime fun and cherished family traditions. The light and airy shortcake pairs perfectly with layers of lemon-marinated strawberries and fresh homemade whipped cream. This recipe is so easy to prepare and SO delicious. The combination of the fluffy and melt-in-your-mouth shortcakes, the burst of flavor from the lemon-marinated strawberries, and the creamy whipped cream is simply irresistible. Whether you're serving it at a family gathering or indulging in a special treat for yourself, this classic strawberry shortcake recipe is sure to bring joy and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried dill, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Pour the marinade over the salmon fillet, making sure to coat it evenly.
  5. Cover the baking sheet with aluminum foil and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Remove the foil and broil the salmon for an additional 2-3 minutes to give it a nice golden crust.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the salmon rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve the baked salmon with your choice of sides and enjoy!
