Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

If you're looking for a simple and delicious way to prepare salmon, this baked salmon recipe is perfect for you! With just a few simple ingredients, you can have a healthy and flavorful meal on the table in no time. The salmon is baked in the oven with a marinade of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and fresh herbs, which gives it a bright and fresh taste. The texture of the salmon is tender and flaky, and it's topped with sliced fresh strawberries for a pop of color and sweetness. This recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion. Try it out and see for yourself why it's one of the best baked salmon recipes out there!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently until it comes together.
  7. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick circle and cut out 6-8 rounds using a biscuit cutter or a drinking glass.
  8. Place the rounds on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. While the shortcakes are baking, prepare the strawberries by tossing them with 1/4 cup of sugar in a bowl.
  10. Let the shortcakes cool for a few minutes before slicing them in half horizontally.
  11. Top each shortcake with a spoonful of whipped cream and a generous amount of sliced strawberries.
  12. Replace the top of the shortcake and add another dollop of whipped cream and more strawberries on top.
  13. Serve immediately and enjoy!
